Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

At Smart Money Flow ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit smartmoneyflow.com.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Name and contact details — when you submit a contact form
  • Comments — when you leave feedback on articles

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website or search terms
  • Device type and operating system

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Send our newsletter (only if you've subscribed)
  • Respond to your inquiries

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic (Google Analytics)
  • Display personalized advertisements (Google AdSense)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analysis

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Some links on our site are affiliate links. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission. This does not affect the price you pay. See our Affiliate Disclosure for details.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
