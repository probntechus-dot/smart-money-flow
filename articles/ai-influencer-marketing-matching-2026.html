AI Influencer Matching: Step-by-Step Campaign Guide

Phase 1: Discovery (Days 1-3)

  1. Define target audience: Age, gender, location, interests, income level
  2. Set campaign goals: Sales, awareness, engagement, app downloads
  3. Determine budget allocation: 70% to influencers, 20% to platform, 10% to content production
  4. Use AI search filters: Engagement rate (4-7% ideal), audience demographics, brand affinities
  5. Generate shortlist: 30-50 potential creators

Phase 2: Vetting (Days 4-5)

  1. Run fraud detection: Check audience quality scores (80%+ genuine followers)
  2. Analyze past content: Brand fit, content quality, messaging alignment
  3. Check engagement authenticity: Comment quality, reply rates, sentiment
  4. Verify audience overlap: Ensure creators don't share too many followers
  5. Narrow to final 10-20 creators

Phase 3: Outreach & Negotiation (Days 6-10)

  1. Automated personalized DMs: AI-generated outreach messages
  2. Share campaign brief: Goals, messaging, content requirements, timeline
  3. Negotiate rates: Micro-influencers: $50-$500/post, mid-tier: $500-$5K, macro: $5K-$50K+
  4. Send contracts: Use platform templates with e-signature
  5. Process payments: Upfront 50% or full payment upon content approval

Phase 4: Content Creation (Days 11-20)

  1. Product/service delivery: Send products or grant access
  2. Creator produces content: Photos, videos, captions
  3. Review and approve: Use platform's approval workflows
  4. Schedule posts: AI suggests optimal times per creator's audience

Phase 5: Launch & Monitor (Days 21-30)

  1. Content goes live: Coordinated multi-creator launch
  2. Real-time tracking: Monitor engagement, clicks, conversions
  3. Sentiment analysis: AI tracks comment sentiment
  4. Adjust messaging: If sentiment is negative, pause and iterate

Phase 6: Analysis & Optimization (Days 31-45)

  1. Calculate ROI: Revenue ÷ total campaign cost
  2. Identify top performers: Which creators drove most conversions?
  3. Analyze content themes: What messaging resonated most?
  4. Build lookalike audiences: Find more creators like top performers
  5. Plan next campaign: Double down on what worked
