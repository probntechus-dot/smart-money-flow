About Me

The person behind Smart Money Flow

AR

Alex Rahman

Pakistani-American Digital Entrepreneur

Hey, I'm Alex 👋

I'm a Pakistani-American digital entrepreneur who's been making money online since 2019. I started Smart Money Flow because I was tired of seeing the same recycled "get rich quick" advice that never actually works.

My journey started with freelancing on Fiverr, where I earned my first $100 offering basic graphic design services. That small win sparked something — I realized that anyone with internet access and determination could build real income online.

Since then, I've built multiple income streams including:

  • Freelancing: Over $150K earned across Upwork and Fiverr
  • Blogging: Multiple blogs generating $3,000+/month combined from ads and affiliates
  • Digital Products: Created courses and templates that sell on autopilot
  • YouTube: A growing channel sharing honest earnings reports
  • Affiliate Marketing: Strategic partnerships earning $2,000+/month

Why Smart Money Flow?

I created this blog with one mission: to share tested, honest, and actionable strategies for making money online. Every method I write about is something I've either personally tried or thoroughly researched.

I'm especially passionate about helping people in South Asia and developing countries access online earning opportunities. Growing up between Pakistan and the US gave me a unique perspective on how powerful the internet can be for creating opportunities regardless of your location.

My Promise to You

  • Honesty first: I'll never recommend something I wouldn't use myself
  • Real numbers: When I share earnings, they're real — with proof when possible
  • Transparency: All affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed
  • Actionable advice: No fluff — every article gives you clear next steps
